Create Artist Playlist
Create and curate your very own Artist Playlist to attach to your Spotify artist account.
1Create A New Playlist
Create a playlist on your Spotify *user* account. This will be the playlist that is attached to your artist account soon.
3Set Playlist As Artist Playlist
Sign into your Spotify For Artists account and set the playlist you have created as your Artist Playlist. By promoting this playlist, you promote all of your music in one spot, making this a valuable marketing strategy.
