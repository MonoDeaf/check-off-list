Message Your Top Listeners On Soundcloud
Establish a deep connection with your power listeners by viewing who is listening to your music the most on Soundcloud. Send a personal message giving appreciation, and let them know of upcoming new music, as they are eager to hear more from you.
1View Your Top Listeners
Visit your SoundCloud fans to find your top listeners and their profiles. From here you can start engaging with your biggest supporters.
Create Your Artist Playlist
Create an artist playlist to display on your Spotify artist profile. Having all of your best music in one playlist can be beneficial for sharing and advertising to new listeners.
1Create A New Playlist
Sign into your Spotify *user* account and create a new playlist. The name of these playlists typically is around 'Artist Mix' or 'Best Of Artist'.
3Add Playlist To Your Spotify Artist Page
Head over to your Spotify for Artist page. In the artist profile tab, select 'Artist Playlist' and choose the playlist you have just created from your user account.
